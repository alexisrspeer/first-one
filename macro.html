Macronutrients

Why Should I Track My Macros?


Minding your diet is extremely important for reaching any type of fitness goal. Most of us have obsessed over tracking calories, but it is more important to track the energy sources in your diet. Macronutrients are your sources of energy: protein, fat, and carbohydrates. Protein & carbohydrates contain 4 calories per gram and fat contains 9 calories per gram. After deciding what your goals are for your body, you can determine the amount of each macronutrient that you should take in. There is no cut and dry, black and white number for a specific gender or age, it all varies, but using a calculator like the one provided, can get you to a great starting point and allow you to make adjustments as you see fit.
